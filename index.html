WELCOME TO PEARL MEDICAL

We envision a future where every healthcare facility has access to reliable and efficient waste management

Why Our work Matters

Every year, millions of Nigerians especially women, children, and health workers are exposed to infections, toxic fumes, and polluted water due to unsafe medical waste disposal. Pearl Medical reduces these risks by providing safe infrastructure, climate-smart innovations, and training that strengthen public health systems while protecting the environment.

Mission Statement

To create a cleaner, safer, and sustainable healthcare ecosystem by providing eco-friendly medical waste solutions powered by technology, innovation, and community empowerment.

Vision Statement

A world where every healthcare facility regardless of location manages waste responsibly, protects the environment, and safeguards human health using smart, inclusive, and sustainable systems.

Key Focus Areas


  • Innovative Technology:
    We develop and deploy innovative technologies, such as the Pearl Medical App and eco-friendly incinerators, to streamline waste management processes and enhance efficiency.

  • Environmental Sustainability:
    We are committed to minimizing our environmental footprint by promoting waste reduction, recycling, and sustainable practices throughout the waste management lifecycle.

  • Community Engagement:
    We prioritize community engagement and capacity building, working closely with healthcare facilities, government agencies, and local stakeholders to raise awareness, build capacity, and foster collaboration.

Our Impact

  • 75% reduction in improper waste disposal practices at supported facilities
  • Over 10,000 health workers trained in safe waste handling
  • Tons of waste diverted from open burning, dumpsites, and water bodies
  • Significant CO₂ emissions avoided through low-emission incineration and recycling
  • Improved facility compliance with national and WHO standards

  • Eco-Friendly Incinerators
  • Pearl Medical App
  • Waste Shredder

Eco-Friendly Medical Waste Incinerators

Our low-emission incinerators are designed for hospitals, primary healthcare centers, and rural clinics that lack safe waste disposal infrastructure. They reduce air pollution, eliminate hazardous waste, and are energy-efficient, durable, and WHO-aligned.

AI-Powered Waste Tracking App

A smart mobile platform that monitors waste from the point of generation to final disposal. It provides real-time data, GPS tracking, compliance reporting, and analytics to help facilities reduce risks and improve accountability.

Plastic Waste Shredders & Recycling

We convert non-infectious medical plastics into reusable materials for local manufacturing industries, reducing landfill waste and supporting circular economy initiatives.

Training & Capacity Building for Health Workers

We equip healthcare workers with the skills to handle waste safely. Our modules cover segregation, infection prevention, PPE use, emergency response, and digital tracking tools.
